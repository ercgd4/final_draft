Home of the Griffins!


The library offers many services for students and teachers. If you would like to utilize any of these services, you may contact the library to set up a time or equipment checkout.
Here at the library we celebrate learning through reading! We can help you find just the right book, resource for research, and introduce you to new and exciting technology!

Computer Lab

Teachers can reserve time in the computer lab for research and writing. Just use the calendar.

Library Time

Teachers can reserve time in the library for research and/or book checkout. Just use the calendar.

iPads

Students and teachers can check out iPads for up to a week at a time. Students must have a signed permission form from your guardian and your classroom teacher. Just send me an email or call extension 4730.

Presentation Equipment

Teachers can reserve classroom projectors for presentations. Just send me an email or call extension 4730.

TV Carts

Teachers can check out TV carts with DVD players for showing videos in your classroom. Just send me an email or call extension 4730.

Videos

Teachers can check out videos from the library to show in class. Just send me an email or call extension 4730.

Library Rules

No one is allowed during open checkout time without a pass (except in the morning before first bell)
Research lab time is to be reserved in advance (teachers, please email me)
Anyone wanting to use computers for research must have signed permission from their teacher
Any printing must be approved by the librarian
Books more than two weeks past due will result in a bill being sent home for the cost of a new copy of the book
